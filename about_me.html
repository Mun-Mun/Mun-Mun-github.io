BIO

My name is Muntadhir (Muhn-tah-deer) Al-Shamri (Al-Shuh-mri), but I go by the name Munti. I am currently attending Indiana University Bloomington majoring in Computer Science, specializing in systems, and minoring in Informatics and Epidemiology. I was born in Mishawaka and raised next door in the city of Elkhart, a small city located up on the state line by Michigan. Being an Arab-American, I visited my parent's birth country many times; Iraq, the country where my father was born, and Morocco, the country where my mother was born. Visiting these countries and seeing the culture firsthand gave me incentive to travel more in the world, especially the South-Eastern Asian countries.
